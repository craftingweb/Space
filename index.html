Blue Origin

Suborbital flight

Blue Origin's New Shepard suborbital rocket on July 20, 2024, for the first time in the company's history, launched a capsule with people into space, with the world's richest man, Jeff Bezos, on board

New Shepard, 18 m tall and 4 m wide, is a reusable spacecraft. The booster unit lifts the pressurized passenger ship to an altitude of about 76 km. The impulse propels the capsule upward into space.

After that, she falls to the ground under three parachutes. The booster rocket is also returning home.
